# Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

### Q: I can't access my Mehmiro account. What should I do?
**A:** 
1. Verify you're using the correct login credentials
2. Check with your school's Mehmiro administrator for account setup
3. Try resetting your password through the login page
4. Contact IT support if you continue having issues

### Q: How do I get the access code for my class assessments?
**A:** Your teacher will provide the access code during class or through your school's communication system. The access code is typically 6-8 characters long (like "ALG3QUIZ").

### Q: Can I take an assessment on my phone or tablet?
**A:** Yes! Mehmiro works on all devices with a web browser. However, for the best experience, we recommend using a computer for longer assessments.

## Taking Assessments

### Q: What happens if I lose internet connection during an assessment?
**A:** Don't worry! Your progress is automatically saved. When you reconnect, you'll be able to continue from where you left off without losing any work.

### Q: Can I change my answers after submitting?
**A:** Once you submit an assessment, you cannot change your answers. However, your teacher may allow multiple attempts, which you can see in the assessment instructions.

### Q: I finished early. Can I leave the assessment room?
**A:** Check with your teacher. Some teachers allow early submission and departure, while others prefer students to review their work during the full time period.

### Q: What if I don't understand a question?
**A:** 
- Look for hints provided by your teacher
- Try to work through the problem step by step
- Remember that guessing is usually better than leaving questions blank
- Ask your teacher for clarification during class time

## Understanding Results

### Q: When will I see my results?
**A:** This depends on your teacher's settings:
- **Immediate**: Results appear right after submission
- **Scheduled**: Results are released at a specific date/time
- **Manual**: Your teacher reviews and releases results manually

### Q: How can I improve my performance?
**A:** 
1. Review the detailed feedback provided
2. Focus on areas identified as needing improvement
3. Ask your teacher for additional help on challenging topics
4. Use the recommended study resources
5. Practice similar problems before the next assessment

## Technical Issues

### Q: The assessment won't load. What should I do?
**A:** 
1. Refresh the page and try again
2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
4. Check your internet connection
5. Contact your teacher or IT support if issues persist

### Q: I'm having trouble with the interface. How do I navigate?
**A:** 
- Use the "Previous/Next" buttons to move between questions
- Use the "Mark for Review" feature for questions you want to revisit
- The progress bar shows your completion status
- Hover over icons for helpful tooltips

### Q: My browser says the site is "not secure." Is this okay?
**A:** Yes, this is normal for educational platforms. Your school has configured the security settings, and your data is protected. You can safely proceed to access your assessments.

## Privacy and Data

### Q: Who can see my assessment results?
**A:** Only you and your teacher(s) can see your individual results. Other students cannot access your personal information or scores.

### Q: What happens to my data after I graduate?
**A:** Your school determines data retention policies. Typically, student data is archived for educational records purposes but is not used for other purposes. Check with your school's privacy policy for specific details.

### Q: Can my parents see my Mehmiro results?
**A:** This depends on your school's settings and your parents' access permissions. Some schools allow parent portals, while others keep student data confidential. Ask your teacher or school administrator.

## For Teachers

### Q: How many students can I add to a class?
**A:** Mehmiro supports classes of any size, but we recommend:
- **Optimal Performance**: Up to 35 students per class
- **Maximum Capacity**: Up to 50 students per class
- **Very Large Classes**: Consider splitting into smaller groups for better individual attention

### Q: Can I reuse questions from previous assessments?
**A:** Yes! You can:
- Copy questions from your existing assessments
- Use the question bank to save favorites
- Import questions from other teachers (with permission)
- Create custom question templates

### Q: How do I handle accommodations for students with special needs?
**A:** Mehmiro supports various accommodations:
- Extended time settings per student
- Text-to-speech compatibility
- Large print formatting
- Alternative question formats
- Custom accessibility settings

Contact your school's accessibility coordinator for specific requirements.

### Q: Can I integrate Mehmiro with our learning management system (LMS)?
**A:** Mehmiro offers integrations with popular LMS platforms including:
- Google Classroom
- Canvas
- Moodle
- Blackboard
- Schoology

Contact your administrator to set up LMS integration.

## Account Management

### Q: How do I update my profile information?
**A:** 
1. Log into your account
2. Click on your profile name/avatar
3. Select "Profile Settings"
4. Update your information and save changes

### Q: Can I change my notification preferences?
**A:** Yes! You can control:
- Email notifications for new assessments
- SMS alerts for due dates
- In-app notifications
- Reminder frequency

### Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
**A:** 
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
