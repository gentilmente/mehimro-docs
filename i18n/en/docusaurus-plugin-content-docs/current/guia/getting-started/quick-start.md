# Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Mehmiro!

This guide will get you up and running with Mehmiro in just a few minutes. Whether you're a teacher or student, we've made the process simple and intuitive.

## For Teachers (2-minute setup)

### What you'll accomplish:
- Create your first class
- Set up a simple assessment
- See your first insights

### The 3-Step Process:

1. **Create Class** (30 seconds)
   - Click "New Class" from your dashboard
   - Fill in basic details (name, subject, grade level)
   - Add students manually or import from your system

2. **Build Assessment** (60 seconds)
   - Choose "Quick Assessment" template
   - Select 5-10 questions from the built-in bank
   - Set time limit and due date

3. **Launch & Monitor** (30 seconds)
   - Share the access code with students
   - Watch real-time progress as they work
   - Get instant results and insights

**Result**: In under 3 minutes, you've created your first data-driven assessment with instant feedback for students and actionable insights for you.

---

## For Students (1-minute setup)

### What you'll accomplish:
- Access your first assessment
- Understand how to use feedback
- Track your learning progress

### The 2-Step Process:

1. **Get Access** (30 seconds)
   - Enter the access code from your teacher
   - Log in with your student credentials
   - Complete your profile

2. **Take Assessment** (30 seconds)
   - Find your assessment in the dashboard
   - Click "Start Assessment"
   - Answer questions and submit

**Result**: In under a minute, you've completed your first Mehmiro assessment and can immediately see your results and personalized feedback.

- ✅ **Instant insights** - See class performance and individual student needs immediately

- ✅ **Better interventions** - Identify struggling students before it's too late
- ✅ **Data-driven decisions** - Make informed choices about instruction and support

### For Students:
- ✅ **Immediate feedback** - Understand what you got right and wrong right away
- ✅ **Personalized guidance** - Get specific recommendations for improvement
- ✅ **Progress tracking** - See your learning journey over time
- ✅ **Reduced anxiety** - Practice with low-stakes, formative assessments
